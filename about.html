Purpose and Functionality:

The sole purpose of this app is to comfortably allow you to switch off any 'screensaving functionality' while, e.g., watching a movie.

You can do that with one click and you can also return to your previous configuration with one click - it's non-destructive.

It achieves this by:

  • disabling screen dimming
  • disabling screen locking
  • disabling the screensaver
